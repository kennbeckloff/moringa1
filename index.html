Lions

Here are some fun facts about lions.

  • Lions weights up to 250kgs
  • A lion's roar can be heard up to 8 km away.
  • Female Lions hunt whilemale lions guard their territory and their cubs.

What to do if a lions sneaks up to you:

  1. Freeze
  2. Don't make eye contact
  3. Wave your arms above your heads and scream loud as you can!
